A Simple SUDOKU Solver
This tool follows a simple iterative approach to solve SUDOKU puzzles. To use to tool:
  1. Reset the SUDOKU Board
  2. Using your mouse or your keyboard, enter the given numbers for the puzzle
  3. Hit the SOLVE button
  4. Print or Save the solution
